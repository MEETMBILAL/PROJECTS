We're Recruiting For

Korean ↔ English Translator

Translate raw manhwa chapters from Korean to natural, engaging English. (Japanese/Chinese also welcome for select projects)

  • Fluent in Korean + excellent English
  • Understanding of webtoon/manhwa tone & slang
  • Can deliver ~1 chapter/week (flexible)

Cleaner / Redrawer

Clean scans (remove text, fix lines) and redraw speech bubbles / SFX when needed.

  • Experience with Photoshop, Clip Studio, or GIMP
  • Good sense of art & lettering flow
  • Attention to detail

Typesetter

Place translated text into bubbles/SFX using fonts that match the original feel.

  • Photoshop / Clip Studio knowledge
  • Good font selection & placement sense
  • Can match vertical / SFX text styles

Proofreader / Quality Checker

Catch grammar, spelling, consistency, and flow issues before release.

  • Strong English grammar & reading speed
  • Enjoy reading manhwa/webtoons
  • Can finish QC in 1–2 days

Raw Provider

Source high-quality raws (preferably official / early access) for upcoming chapters.

  • Access to Korean platforms (Kakao, Naver, Lezhin…)
  • Reliable & fast

Other Roles

Discord moderator, thumbnail artist, project manager, uploader, social media helper...

If you're passionate — we can find a spot for you!
